HOW TO PLAY My DICE GAME
STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

Playing the My Dice cash game on our website offers an exciting opportunity to test your luck and strategy while having the chance to win amazing prizes. By following this step-by-step guide, you can dive into the world of online My Dice and experience the thrill of this traditional game with a modern twist.

STEP 1: ACCESSING THE PLATFORM

  • Open your preferred web browser and visit our website, or download our My Dice app.
  • If you're new to our website, sign up for an account using your email or mobile number and complete the registration process.

STEP 2: NAVIGATING TO My Dice

  • Once logged in, navigate through the platform's game offerings and find the real My Dice game.

STEP 3: PLACING BETS

  • Upon entering the My Dice game, you'll be presented with a virtual betting table. Different bet amounts will be displayed, allowing you to choose your preferred symbol.
  • This game involves six dice, each with six sides, and each side has a symbol on it. The gamer should predict which symbol, out of the six, will show face up many times after a roll. The symbols include:
    • HORSE
    • LION
    • PANDA
    • ELEPHANT
    • DEER
    • MONKEY
  • Click on the desired chip value to select your bet amount.
  • Place your bet by clicking on the area of the table that corresponds to the outcome you're betting on.

STEP 4: ROLLING THE DICE

  • After placing your bet, press the 'Roll' button to initiate the dice roll.
  • The symbols determine the outcome of the game.

STEP 5: UNDERSTANDING WINNING COMBINATIONS

  • Each symbol on the dice represents a different value.
  • The game's primary objective is to predict which symbols will appear face-up the most.
  • Payouts are based on the number of times a chosen symbol appears face-up among all the dice.

STEP 6: WINNING AND PAYOUTS

  • If your chosen symbol appears on one or more dice, you win based on the odds associated with that symbol.
  • Payouts vary depending on the rarity of the symbol's appearance. Common symbols offer smaller payouts, while rare symbols yield higher rewards.

STEP 7: COLLECTING YOUR WINNINGS

  • If you win, the winnings will be instantly credited to your our website account.
  • You can use these winnings to play more rounds of the My Dice King game or other slots on our platform.
